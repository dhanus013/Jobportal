About Meridian Hiring

A simpler way to apply

Meridian Hiring is a lightweight job application portal built to make applying for a role — and reviewing applications — as straightforward as possible.

For candidates

Submit your details and resume in under two minutes. No account required, no lengthy forms — just your name, contact info, the role you're applying for, and a PDF resume. You'll get a confirmation with a reference ID the moment your application is received.

For recruiters

Every application lands in a secure dashboard, with resumes stored privately and accessible only through authenticated, time-limited links. Filter, review, and track candidates without spreadsheets or email threads.

How it works

  1. Candidate fills out the application form and uploads a resume (PDF, up to 5MB).
  2. The submission is securely stored and instantly visible to recruiters.
  3. Recruiters review, sort, and manage applications from the dashboard.
